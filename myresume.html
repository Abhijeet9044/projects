Many people struggle with the concept of self-love. I can totally understand this as we are often bombarded with different examples of how our lives are “supposed” to be. You were born this way for a reason — because you are unique! No two people will ever be the same, so there is no point in trying to be someone else. Your version of success should be about what is important to you, and not about what other people are doing.
